Pizza

Description

A pizza pie is a simple and delicious flatbread pie consisting of sauce and cheese and various toppings. Today we are going to create a simple cheese pizza with no toppings

Toppings

  • Pizza Crust - 8 inch
  • Tomato Sauce - 6 tablespoons
  • Mozzarella Cheese - 1 cup
  • Dried Herbs - 1 tablespoon

Steps

  1. Defrost the frozen pizza crust by thawing it at room temperature for 6 hours
  2. Preheat the oven to 450 Fahrenheit
  3. Spread the tmoate sauce on the thawed crust
  4. Spread the grated mozzarella cheese evenly over the tomato sauce
  5. Place the pie in the over and bake for 30 minutes
  6. Finally, broil the pizza for 5 minutes to get a golden brown cheese top
  7. Pizza is ready to enjoy!
